Music

Bass Lemon

2014-2020

Bass Lemon was a funk-rock band. I started Bass Lemon with my friends when I was a teenager. During the time we were active we played at venues in and around Fremantle and Perth. We also played at Nannup and Fairbridge music festivals.

Cerauno

2021-Present

My bass playing friend, Fred, got me involved in Cerauno after the previous keyboard player left. Cerauno is more of a soul/R&B/funk kind of deal. We haven't record much yet but I'm hoping to change that soon.
